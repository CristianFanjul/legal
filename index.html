Privacy Policy for Nocturne Slash

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Nocturne Slash (hereinafter "the Game") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

The Game does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, address, or phone number to play. However, we collect certain technical data automatically to improve your experience:

  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous information about how players interact with the Game (e.g., game duration, levels reached, performance metrics).
  • Technical and Device Information: Such as device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to receive anonymous reports when the Game crashes, helping us fix software bugs.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To monitor and analyze game performance and usage trends.
  • To provide technical support and fix software bugs.
  • To improve the Game features and user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you on their own platforms. These include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)
  • AdMob (Future advertising implementation)

4. Data Retention

We do not store your personal information on our own servers. The technical data collected by Firebase is stored by Google in accordance with their privacy policies.

5. User Rights (Data Deletion)

Although we do not collect direct personal information, users may request the deletion of technical identifiers and event data associated with their device by sending an email to cristian_polar@hotmail.com. We will process these requests in accordance with the tools provided by Google Firebase.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at:
